# Language QA Gates

Use this checklist after localization and before final export.

## Gates

- Terminology matches the glossary for all locked terms.
- Formality and tone match the style guide across lessons, quizzes, and emails.
- Cultural adaptation checklist items are all resolved.
- Examples, units, dates, currency, and regulations are localized appropriately.
- Quizzes remain standalone and do not reference the source language.
- No mixed-language fragments remain in lessons, quizzes, or emails.

## Output

Record PASS or FAIL with brief notes in:
`<course-folder>/localization/localization-qa-report.md`
