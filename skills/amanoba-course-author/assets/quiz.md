# Quiz (lesson pool, minimum 7)

## Rules (non-negotiable)

- Standalone: no references to the lesson/day/module/course (“in this lesson”, “today”, “Day X”, “as described above”).
- Exactly 1 correct answer.
- Author at least 4 options (1 correct + at least 3 plausible distractors). Platform may display 3 (1 correct + 2 random wrong).
- No “all of the above” / “none of the above”, no trick wording, avoid double negatives.

## Minimum blueprint (7 questions)

1. Definition check (1)
2. Disambiguation between similar concepts (1)
3. Application scenario (2)
4. Do vs don't decision (1)
5. Error spotting / diagnosis (1)
6. Metric or success criteria (1)

## Authoring format (human-readable)

**Question:** [Standalone question]

A) [Option]
B) [Option]
C) [Option]
D) [Option]

**Correct:** [A/B/C/D]

**Why correct:** [1–2 sentences]
**Why others are wrong:**
- A: [1 line]
- B: [1 line]
- C: [1 line]
- D: [1 line]
